VEGGIE STIR-FRY

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup snap peas
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp salt

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add vegetables & garlic, cook for 5 mins.
  3. Stir in soy sauce & broth.
  4. Simmer for 3 mins.
  5. Serve hot.
